Chicken soup

Description

This is simply a chiken soup. In Russia it calls "bul'on"

Ingredients

  • chiken (whole or pieces)
  • onion
  • carrot
  • laurel leaf
  • peppercorns
  • salt
  • parsley

Steps

  1. put chicken into the pot and fill it the water then cook it
  2. clean onion and carrot
  3. cut the onion onto two pieces
  4. cut carrot onto 3 or 4 flat pieces
  5. after the water in the pot is boiling remove foam and reduce the fire on minimun
  6. heat the frying pan
  7. fry onion and carrot without sunflower oil
  8. put onion, carrot, laurel leaf, parsly and salt into the pot
  9. cool two hours
