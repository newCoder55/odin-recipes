Salad Obzhorka

Description

This is a recipe of salad. It's really nutritious.

Ingredients

  • boiled chiken meat
  • onion
  • carrot
  • salty (marinated) pickles
  • garlic
  • salt, pepper
  • sunflower oil
  • mayonnaise

Steps

  1. cut chicken meat
  2. cut the onin onto small pieces
  3. fry onion with sunflower oil
  4. grate carrot by big(medium) grater
  5. fry carrot with sunflower oil
  6. cut pickles onto small pieces
  7. cut garlic onto really small pieces
  8. put all together, salt, pepper
  9. mix with mayonnaise
