Rasolnik

Description

This is a recipe of soup. It's really popular in Russia.

Ingredients

  • chiken soup
  • onion
  • carrot
  • potatoes
  • rice
  • salty (marinated) pickles
  • sunflower oil

Steps

  1. soak the rice
  2. clean potatoes and cut it onto small pieces
  3. reheat chiken soup
  4. put rice and popatoes into the pot
  5. cut the onin onto small pieces
  6. fry onion with sunflower oil and put it into the pot
  7. grate carrot by big(medium) grater
  8. fry carrot with sunflower oil and put it into the pot
  9. cut pickles onto small pieces
  10. fry pickles with sunflower oil and put it into the pot
  11. cook 15 minutes
